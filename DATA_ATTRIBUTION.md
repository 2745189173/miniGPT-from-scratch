# Data Attribution

The examples and published experiments use the Tiny Shakespeare corpus from
Andrej Karpathy's `char-rnn` repository:

https://github.com/karpathy/char-rnn/tree/master/data/tinyshakespeare

The corpus contains works by William Shakespeare, whose original texts are in
the public domain. The dataset is downloaded at runtime by
`scripts/download_data.py` and is intentionally excluded from this repository.

Users who replace the corpus are responsible for verifying the license and
usage terms of their own data.
